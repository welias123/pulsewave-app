Why Pulsewave

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🎵

Massive Library

Stream virtually any song in existence. Powered by the world's largest music index.

🎛️

7-Band Equalizer

Fine-tune every frequency. Boost the bass, cut the highs, or pick from 6 built-in presets — Flat, Bass Boost, Vocal, Rock, Electronic, Classical.

📝

Playlists

Create unlimited playlists, add any track, reorder, rename, and delete. Your library stays saved locally.

♥

Liked Songs

Heart any track to save it instantly. Your liked songs are always one click away.

🔒

Private by Default

Accounts are local — no cloud, no tracking, no data sent anywhere. Your music stays yours.

⚡

Shuffle & Repeat

Full queue management with shuffle mode, repeat all, and repeat one. Control your listening experience.

Get Started

Download Pulsewave

Free forever. Available for Windows and Linux.

Windows

Windows 10 / 11
x64 · NSIS Installer

Download for Windows v1.3.0 · 120 MB · ZIP (entpacken + Pulsewave.exe starten)

Linux

Ubuntu 20.04+ / Debian
x64 · AppImage

Coming Soon Linux AppImage · in Kürze verfügbar

iPhone / iPad

iOS Safari · Kein App Store
Installierbar als Web-App

1 Starte node server.js auf deinem PC
2 Öffne http://<PC-IP>:3000 in Safari
3 Tippe Teilen → Zum Home-Bildschirm
Läuft im selben WLAN wie dein PC · v1.3.0

Quick Setup

1
ZIP herunterladen — Die ZIP-Datei entpacken (z.B. auf den Desktop)
2
Pulsewave.exe starten — Einfach doppelklicken, kein Installer nötig
3
Create an account — Local-only, no email needed
4
Start listening — Search any song and hit play 🎵
